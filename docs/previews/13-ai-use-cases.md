It's 3 AM. Your phone buzzes: **"CRITICAL: Production database server — disk failure detected."** You roll out of bed, VPN in, and start the familiar dance. Replace the failed disk, rebuild the RAID array, restore from backup. By 7 AM, you've been at it for four hours.

Six months later, you deploy a different approach. A lightweight ML model monitors SMART data from every disk in the fleet. One Tuesday afternoon, it flags a disk: **"Predicted failure within 72 hours — confidence: 94%."** You order a replacement, schedule the swap during business hours, and replace it with zero downtime. Full night's sleep.

That's the shift this chapter is about. You already understand compute, networking, storage, monitoring, and automation. Now you'll learn where AI plugs into each of those domains to make your work fundamentally better.
